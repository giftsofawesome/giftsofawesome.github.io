Welcome to Gifts Of Awesome!

We aim to create an active community of teens who are set to make change and improve the world in their own, unique ways. Gifts of Awesome is a platform that encourages teens to identify a cause in their community or abroad, and solve it. Period.

Mission to Ukraine

Leaders Karina and Leonard Melnik (New York) raised money to provide clothing for a family in Vinnitsa, Ukraine. The head provider and father in the Burlaka family was recently killed in a war, and was left to battle the harsh winter without the proper clothing. Gifts of Awesome helped to improve their condition by gathering funding for clothing, and the kids are doing great!
