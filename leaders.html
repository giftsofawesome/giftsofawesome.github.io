Our Leaders

Mission to Ukraine

Leaders Karina and Leonard Melnik (New York) raised money to provide clothing for a family in Vinnitsa, Ukraine. The Burlaka family's head provider and father was recently killed in war, leaving the family to battle the harsh winter without proper clothing. Gifts of Awesome improved their condition by gathering funding for clothing. The kids are doing great!

Water for Africa

Peter Mikhlin set out to increase access to water for remote villages throughout the Africa.

Girls United

Leader: Dea Kurti. Girls United is a new initiative from GOA that focuses on women empowerment by sending books focusing on women's gender equality to developing countries. We have one shipment of books being send over to Cuba soon.

Maternity and Paternity Leave

Leaders: Mariya Samofalova and Amy Zaretsky. Our goal is to raise awareness about maternity and paternity leave in the U.S. through collaboration and partnership with people that support our cause to increase awareness.

Classroom Supplies for Africa

Leaders: Nana Kiziriya, Karina Melnik, and Aimee Trivino. We are dedicated to promoting education and learning amongst young children in Nigeria! We will be working together to create drives and fundraisers throughout the year.

Aiding the Elderly

Leaders: Jamie Kwan and Helen Huang. Join this initiative if you love to make people smile! We organize visits to Nursing Homes and Senior Centers, make fun gifts, and select students to perform for the elderly. By joining this initiative, you will be able to help out in scheduling visits, recruiting performers, and planning out activities.
