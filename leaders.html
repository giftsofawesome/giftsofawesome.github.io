Our Leaders

Mission to Ukraine

Leaders Karina and Leonard Melnik (New York) raised money to provide clothing for a family in Vinnitsa, Ukraine. The Burlaka family's head provider and father was recently killed in war, leaving the family to battle the harsh winter without proper clothing. Gifts of Awesome improved their condition by gathering funding for clothing. The kids are doing great!

Water for Africa

Peter Mikhlin set out to increase access to water for remote villages throughout the Africa.

Girls United

Leader: Dea Kurti. Girls United is a new initiative from GOA that focuses on women empowerment by sending books focusing on women's gender equality to developing countries. We have one shipment of books being send over to Cuba soon.

Maternity and Paternity Leave

Leaders: Mariya Samofalova and Amy Zaretsky. Our goal is to raise awareness about maternity and paternity leave in the U.S. through collaboration and partnership with people that support our cause to increase awareness.

Classroom Supplies for Africa

Leaders: Nana Kiziriya, Karina Melnik, and Aimee Trivino. We are dedicated to promoting education and learning amongst young children in Nigeria! We will be working together to create drives and fundraisers throughout the year.

Smile! Senior Center Visits

Leaders: Jamie Kwan and Helen Huang. Join this initiative if you love to make people smile! We organize visits to Nursing Homes and Senior Centers, make fun gifts, and select students to perform for the elderly. By joining this initiative, you will be able to help out in scheduling visits, recruiting performers, and planning out activities.

Righting the Wrong of Littering

Leader: Harry Rodus. He aims to end the mentality that littering "just once" is fine. If everyone litters with this same mentality, they leave behind much more garbage than they think.

Bright : Hospital Visits

Join this initiative to help brighten up the days of patients at hospitals. In this initiative, we will do different activities with the patients and try to get to know their personal story. Different roles include planning out activities, scheduling visits, and helping to run activity sessions.

PadPals

Leaders: Yuki Fang and Tiffiny Lee. Homeless women should not be forced to choose between a meal and a pad. Join our initiative, PadPals, if you want to stop the stigma around menstruation and give the necessary sanitary pads and tampons every homeless woman deserves in NYC.

Operation Smile: Cancer Patients

Leader: Jenny Liang He. Join this initiative if you like to make people smile! This operation is targeted at young cancer patients. We organize visits to Medical Centers, interact with young children, tutor them, and make gifts for them as well! Drives and fundraisers will be created in order to benefit the patients. By joining this operation, you will be able to earn club credit and be able to help out in scheduling visits, be in charge of fundraisers, plan activities, etc!

Bike Locks for Safe Bikes

Many people from all around the world have probably experienced difficulty with bike safety. Bikes are stolen incredibly often around the world and around our neighborhood. Help create bike locks that are affordable and easy to use. These locks will prevent crimes like this from happening, make calls to police and insurance providers significantly shorter and will be sold at a very low price. Join today if you want to help this idea come to life and want to make a difference in your community and even the world.
