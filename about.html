About Us

Gifts of Awesome is a not-for-profit organization that is dedicated to encouraging teenagers to identify a volunteering cause around them, and pursue it. GOA creates leaders, and provides them with the resources, skills, and connections necessary in their own, unique initiatives. As a service to our members and leaders, Gifts of Awesome connects members with professionals in a wide variety of fields. We are scheduled to host live talks with Politicians, Nobel Laureates, and other esteemed individuals.
